#### When that tail starts thumping, you'd better be ready.

---

_thump. thump. thumP. thuMP. thUMP. tHUMP. THUMP. THUMP THUMP THUMP!_  
That could only mean one thing. Incoming pets.  

_THUMP THUMP THUMP! THUMP. tHUMP. thUMP. thuMP. thumP. thump. thump._  
Straining against every fiber, the thumping slowed into a patter. 

Damn it. Come on, human. Bring it on.

A hand raised, slowly coming over. 

_Focus. It’s coming._

A second hand raised, coming. Omigod. This is gonna be the GOOD stuff. 

Fingers landing, reaching around to scratch, and… _SMOOCH_.

KISS?? For _me_? 

Ears pinned back, eyes closed. Heaven. 

More. More. _MORE_. 

The hands delivered. _Scratch. Smooch. Scratch. Smooch. SCRATCH. SMOOCH._

_SMOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOCH!_

And then…

_Pat. Pat. Pat._

Hands retreated. 

A blink. A pause.

_thump. thump. thumP. thuMP. thUMP. tHUMP. THUMP. THUMP THUMP THUMP!_

Round two, anyone?
